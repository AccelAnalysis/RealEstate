Service Pillar

Farm & Deal Radar™

Move beyond “we sent mail to this zip code.” Farm & Deal Radar gives you a structured way to define, compare, and work a set of micro-farms that actually match your goals.

  • Smart Farm Design: We help you define farms using turnover, price bands, property age, and owner profiles.
  • Micro-Farm Priorities: Each farm is scored and tiered, so you know where to go deeper and where to simply monitor.
  • Time-Phased Campaigns: Outreach waves that align with seasonality, inventory cycles, and your capacity.
  • Performance Feedback: Tracking that tells you which farms are producing conversations, listings, and deals.

Deliverable: a farm playbook that shows where to focus, how often to touch, and what to measure for both short-term listings and long-term positioning.

Ownership & Investor Intelligence™

Knowing the property is one thing. Knowing the person behind it is what unlocks off-market opportunities, repeat deals, and focused conversations.

  • Owner & Investor Lists: We compile and structure who owns what, where, across your chosen markets.

Result: a clearer picture of your local ownership and investor landscape, and a way to intentionally build relationships with the people that fit your business.
